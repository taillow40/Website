Welcome to Statisticize!
This is a game about knowing your opponent.

Go to the link: https://taillow40.github.io/Website/UniqueID
Use the same UniqueID as the people you're playing with.

The getquestion button will give a new question and whether the high answer or low answer will win, only one person needs to click the button!

Each person thinks about their answer and then goes around in a circle saying their answer, whoever has the corresponding highest or lowest answer wins and gains a point.
