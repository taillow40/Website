Professional
In my time at Harbourvest, just like in my personal life I was always looking for ways to improve processes, often through automation.
Automated Office365 Deprovisoining
  • The furthest reaching project I completed, along side a teammate I wrote a set of scripts to automate deprovisoining of O365 licenses
  • The script marks all inactive users that should not have licenses, then requests approval from their managers to remove the license and finally removes the license if applicable
  • When I finished the internship, the script had already reclaimed 85 licenses, saving the company $46,000 a year
  • Additionally completed the Six Sigma Certification through this project
GSD Portal
  • In our office room, there was a large screen on the wall that was kept off
  • Seeing that screen, I built a dashboard that displays information about our work that we could display on it
  • The GSDportal displays our current ticket queue, as well as what regions are currently online
Insight Auto Approval
  • Insight is the external system Harbourvest uses to send Work From Home kits
  • I created a script that streamlines the process, turning a 2 step process down to a 1 step process for my manager.
PowerBI Dashboard Component
  • On a powerBI dashboard, I created a component to display the number of incidents per region per person
  • This component was created by combining the data from our ticket logs with our Workday logs
